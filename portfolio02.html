Original Imagined Campaign

"You're a Natural" Campaign

PROBLEM

As the quality of smartphone cameras skyrockets thanks to software and automatic settings, the jump from casual to enthusiast photographers is getting increasingly difficult.

SOLUTION

Canon will invite the 'Casuals' to enter the mindset of an enthusiast, where the value proposition of a standalone camera becomes clear.

APP + FILTERS

Canon will create the Natural Photographer app that allows users to have more control over their image, and shoot in the closest format to RAW.

By giving quick tips, and breaking down in a simple way what's going on 'under the hood', the Casuals will be gradually exposed to new photography techniques and ideas.

This app will also demonstrate the usefulness of being able to control individual settings, and will show the limitations of the phone's sensor and cameras.

OUT OF HOME

Dotted in various interesting places in select cities, branded #naturalfilter installations that act as smartphone 'tripods', opening up new ideas for angles and concepts to try out.

With an added window containing a UV filter or a polarising filter, the installation will also demonstrate the power of controlling an image before it reaches the camera's sensor.

GOLDEN HOUR WATCH FACE

Golden hour refers to a one-hour time period after sunrise and before sunset that basks the earth in a complimentary, stunning glow.

As this window is different every day, Canon will create a branded watch face that allows users to keep track, and act as a prompt to take beautiful photographs.

PHONE CASE FILTERS

As an added phone accessory, Canon can team up with Otterbox to offer limited edition transparent Filter Cases that offer phone users a polarising and a UV filter on either end - keeping the Casuals in that enthusiast mindset every time they turn on their camera.
